Primary action = gold · second voice = sapphire

Gold (--ep-accent) marks every primary action in both themes; a solid gold fill always carries dark ink (--ep-accent-on). Sapphire (--ep-second) is the brand's second voice and headline flourish — never the primary button.

Semantic surface & text roles

Brand palette (fixed across themes)

Sapphire, gold/yolk, paper, slate and ink scales keep their hex in both themes — only which stop each semantic role points at changes.

Typography — Roboto (display + body) & JetBrains Mono

One sans family carries the whole hierarchy through size, weight and tracking. The bold sapphire flourish lands on the second phrase of a display headline via <em>.
